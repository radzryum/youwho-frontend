Safety Guidelines

Your physical and emotional safety is our absolute highest priority. YouWho is a platform for peer support, not professional crisis intervention.

If You Are in Immediate Crisis

If you or someone else is in immediate physical danger, or experiencing a mental health emergency, please immediately contact professional help or local emergency services.

Reporting Bad Behavior

We maintain a zero-tolerance policy for harassment, bullying, or inappropriate content. Because video feeds are NOT recorded for privacy reasons, we rely on users to skip and report bad actors.

  • If you feel uncomfortable, click the "Skip" (Next Peer) button immediately.
  • Do not share personally identifiable information (real name, address, school).
  • Do not send or request money.

Community Standards

We are building a sanctuary for the local community and beyond. Every user is expected to contribute to a culture of empathy and respect.

How to be a Great Listener

  • Listen to Understand, Not to Reply: Give the speaker the space to finish their thoughts without interrupting.
  • Validate Feelings: Use phrases like "That sounds incredibly stressful" instead of "Don't worry about it."
  • Avoid Fixing: Most people just want to be heard, not given immediate solutions to their problems.

How to be a Great Speaker

  • Be Respectful of Boundaries: If a listener seems overwhelmed, ask if they are okay to continue the topic.
  • Utilize the Cards: If you aren't sure what to say, use the prompt cards at the bottom of the screen to guide your thoughts.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

Team P.O.O.C.H. deeply respects your privacy. Because the core feature of YouWho! is anonymous emotional support, we collect the bare minimum amount of data required to operate the service.

What We Don't Collect

  • Video & Audio: Your WebRTC video and audio streams are encrypted end-to-end. We do not record, store, or analyze your video calls.
  • Real Identity: When you create an Anonymous Alias, your real name is never tied to your public profile.

What We Do Collect

  • Authentication Data: Your email address (via Google Auth or manual signup) is stored securely via Firebase strictly for login and account recovery purposes.
  • Session Data: We temporarily track your chosen "Mood" and "Topic" strictly to route you through our matchmaking algorithm. This data is discarded when the session ends.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is YouWho! really free?

Yes. Every user receives free, anonymous 20-minute support sessions. We also offer a Premium tier that unlocks unlimited time and access to verified professional guides.

Is my conversation being recorded?

Absolutely not. The video and audio feeds use WebRTC technology, meaning the connection is directly between you and your peer. The data never passes through a recording server.

How does the matchmaking work?

We use a strict logical algorithm. We pair one "Speaker" with one "Listener" who have both selected the exact same Emotional State and chosen the same conversational Topic. We do not use AI sentiment analysis; we rely on your chosen inputs.

What if I match with a troll?

Click the yellow "Next Peer" button on your control panel to immediately disconnect and be thrown back into the queue for a new, safer match.
